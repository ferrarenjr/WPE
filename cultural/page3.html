SANDUROT EXHIBIT

What?: On September 27 2024, I went to Robinsons Dumaguete and viewed the Sandurot exhibit displayed in the malls grounds, I looked at the costumes and the pictures of the sandurot festival that took place in Dumaguete.

So what?: This exhibit showed me the culture behind the festival and saw something beautiful in it, it shows the history and the meaning of the festival. The exhibit captured the true essence of the festival

Now what?: This exhibit helped me understand the true purpose of the festival and its beauty

Because of this activity, I know more about the sandurot festival and am updated on the festivals in Dumaguete
